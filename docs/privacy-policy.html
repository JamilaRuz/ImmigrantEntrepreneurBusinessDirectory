Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

Thank you for using our Immigrant Entrepreneur Canada ("App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address (personal and company)

Company Information:

  • Company name
  • Services provided
  • Physical address
  • Phone number

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your business listing in the directory
  • Enable other users to discover, contact, and connect with your business
  • Facilitate communication and networking among entrepreneurs
  • Improve the overall functionality and user experience of the App

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

All the information you provide, including personal and company details, will be publicly available to any user who downloads or accesses the App. This includes:

  • Displaying your company profile and contact information in the directory
  • Allowing other users to view and potentially reach out to you directly

We do not sell your information to third parties. However, since your information is visible to other users, it may be accessible to third parties through the public directory.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Database Protection:
Our databases are secured and stored in environments protected by firewalls and authentication systems.

Data Minimization & Limitation:
We only collect the data necessary for your listing to function properly in the directory.

Regular Security Updates:
We keep our backend services and frameworks up to date with the latest security patches and fixes.

Monitoring and Logging:
Access and changes to sensitive user data may be logged and monitored to detect unauthorized behavior.

5. Your Choices

  • You may review and update your personal and company information at any time through your account settings.
  • If you wish to remove your information from the directory, you may contact us at admin@immigrantentrepreneurcanada.ca or use the app's deletion feature if available.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App, and the updated version will be effective as of the "Effective Date" above.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: admin@immigrantentrepreneurcanada.ca
